Design Notes

Palette: Sandy background (#F8F4EE), terracotta primary (#B85A2C), warm amber accent (#E8A246). Feels like a vacation journal — approachable, not corporate.

Fonts: Outfit (headings, logo) + DM Sans (body) — both Google Fonts, free.

Status badges: Blue = Considering, Red = Eliminated, Green = Booked. Pill-shaped, muted tints — readable at a glance.

Both views ship: A = browsing/adding phase · B = decision phase. Toggle between them from the nav.
